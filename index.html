The Founder's
Philosophy

Manish Tomar is a distinguished systems architect whose career is anchored in the pursuit of absolute operational precision. As the visionary behind Kavish Informatics, he has bridged the critical gap between complex IT hardware and seamless business continuity. With a specialization in court-side automation for international sports networks and industrial-scale infrastructure, Manish has spent over a decade designing environments that demand zero latency and 100% reliability.

His leadership style is defined by "The Architecture of Discipline"—a belief that whether one is managing a high-throughput server farm or a premium kitchen line, the core systems must be unshakeable. This multidisciplinary mastery allows him to manage the digital backbone of major organizations while simultaneously pioneering innovation in the food and beverage industry through The Mom's Kitchen.

Executive Brief

The Mom's
Kitchen

The Mom’s Kitchen is a masterclass in culinary precision, founded by Manish Tomar to harmonize traditional soul-food with the efficiency of modern systems. Located in the industrial heart of Okhla, the brand provides an elevated breakfast and snack experience for discerning professionals who refuse to compromise on health or heritage.

By integrating Systemic SOPs—standardized operating procedures typically found in high-tech manufacturing—Manish has eliminated the variables that usually compromise street-side food quality. Every meal, from our signature Indori Poha to our crispy Masala Dosas, follows a rigorous "Recipe Architecture" that ensures consistency, hygiene, and unparalleled nutrition. Utilizing a Digital-First Operations Model, the result is a sanctuary for authentic Indian taste, where every plate is a commitment to purity and every process is an example of technological finesse.
